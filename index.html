Welcome!

If you are here you are probably interested in "Fear of Those" This web series/comic is about a world that has been invaded by demons and monsters. Each one has a unique and special ability.

Kris

Kris is a girl is often anxious and doesn't socializes to much, she is very observant and likes silly jokes.

Marshall

Marshall is an ex-policeman that has an addiction to coffee. He is often sarcastic and with a special sense of humor.

Vincent

Vincent is a wery powerful demon that takes care of Diego. He is very interested in fashion and art.

Diego

Just a ghost that acts like a normal kid. He has always energy and he only trusts in Vincent and his friends

Dylan

A very silly extroverted guy that also likes jokes.
